About

Concept

The Concept we came up with was creating a 3D Art Exhibtion. Created on a simple website. the reason behind this was because Lando & Maxim came up with the idea, based of an inspiration they found when they were exploring different ideas and the stumbled upon ThreeJS and the possibilities that came with ThreeJS. So the concept itself is an Art Exhibition room where u can interact with different 3D Art models.

ThreeJS

So as said in our concept we used ThreeJS to create out Art Room. We wont go in depth on each attribute and how we used it, but to explain shortly. ThreeJS is a javascript pluggin so trough coding u can get a variety of different results. You can animate a variety of different effects but we used it to create our pedestals, make the 3D models move up and down and every little animation and effect is all due to JS or ThreeJs. This job was for Maxim.

P5

We used P5 for the animated background that u can see on our homepage. We went looking for a variety of different interactions and animations that could have made our website more interesting but in the end we landed on this because we felt like it added to the abstract part of our webiste. It also has a tiny hidden interactive feature, in which u can press the "S" button on your homepage and in doing so u will create screenshot. All of the P5 coding and adjustments were lando his job, As also everything u can seen on the home page. For example the intro and interactions.
